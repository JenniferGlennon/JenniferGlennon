Welcome to the Birkbeck Babylab!

We're a group of researchers based at the Centre for Brain and Cognitive Development in London. We use top of the range technologies to study infant and child development.

We want to better understand how children learn and develop. We are also interested in how and why some children develop conditions such as autism.
